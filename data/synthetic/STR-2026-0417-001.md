## 4. Red Flags

### 4.1 Structuring (MAS 626 para 6.4)
Four inbound wires arrived within 22 days, each sized immediately below SGD 500,000. Treated by the bank as a single aggregated transaction under para 6.4.

### 4.2 Shell company counterparties with opaque ownership
All four originators are British Virgin Islands entities routed through the same Geneva correspondent bank. The ordering bank did not disclose beneficial ownership. Subject has not provided a satisfactory explanation of the commercial rationale for receiving funds from these entities.

### 4.3 Mismatch with declared source of wealth
Subject declared "investment advisory fees" as source of wealth. Arrowmark Capital Advisors' ACRA-filed revenues for FY2024 were SGD 340,000. The inbound volume in Q4 2025 alone is ~5.8× full-year declared firm revenue.

### 4.4 Relationship between subject and originators not established
Subject was unable, on telephone enquiry of 2026-03-28, to describe the nature of his relationship with Nielsen Enterprises, Nescoll, or Hangon. He stated only that the transfers were "for a family investment". He did not name a beneficial owner.

## 5. Information Gap (Where We Need Help)
